Hongming Zhang

Biography

I am currently a senior research scientist at Tencent AI Lab, Seattle. Before that, I was a research scholar at University of Pennsylvania, working with Prof. Prof. Dan Roth. In 2021, I got my Ph.D. degree from HKUST, supervised by Prof. Yangqiu Song. Before that, I received my MPhil. degree from the TLE program, HKUST in 2018 and B.E.degree from the ECE department, HKUST in 2016. My research interest is primarily on commonsense reasoning and multi-modal event understanding. Specifically, I aim at building a large-scale eventuality-centric knowledge graph to connect different modalities and help machines understand the event-centric commonsense. You can find me at hzhangal [AT] cse.ust.hk or learn more about me with my Google Scholar, Github, and CV (Aug, 2021).
